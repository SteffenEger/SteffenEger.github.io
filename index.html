Steffen Eger

Hi there. I am currently Independent Research Group Leader (IRGL) at TU Darmstadt.
My research focus is on Natural Language Processing (NLP) and currently, I am particularly interested in evaluation metrics, cross-lingual approaches, cross-temporal approaches (language change) and applications in argument mining, metaphor and emotion analysis, and poetry.
Earlier research interests included alignments, opinion dynamics (in a social science/economic context) as well as applications in math (such as integer compositions and limits of powers of matrices).
